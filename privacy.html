Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

1. Overview

Alien Project is a personal content automation tool. This Privacy Policy explains what information is accessed and how it is used.

2. Information We Access

When you authorize the App via TikTok OAuth, the App may access basic profile information (user ID, display name) and permission to upload and publish videos via the video.upload and video.publish scopes.

3. How Information Is Used

Accessed information is used solely to authenticate the App with TikTok and upload and publish video content to the authorized TikTok account. No personal data is sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising.

4. Data Storage

OAuth access tokens are stored locally on the developer's own machine in a private configuration file. No data is stored on external servers controlled by this App.

5. Data Retention

Tokens are retained only as long as needed to maintain the TikTok integration. You can revoke access at any time via TikTok's app settings at tiktok.com/settings/connected-apps.

6. Third-Party Services

This App integrates with TikTok's API. TikTok's own Privacy Policy applies to data processed by TikTok.
